Spotify App Installation Guide

Installation Steps for iPhone

  1. Open the App Store and go to the profile in the top right corner
    • Scroll to the bottom of the screen and press "Sign Out"
  2. Press "Sign in with Apple ID"
    • On the screen that appears, press the middle button
  3. Enter login information
    E-mail: spotify.rus@yandex.ru
    Password: XXXXXX
  4. Click "Other features"
    • Select "Do not improve"
    • There is no need to save the password
    • Login information is valid for 30 minutes only
  5. Install the Spotify application
    • It will download and appear on the main screen of the phone
  6. Log into your Apple ID again
    • Click "Log Out" at the bottom of the profile screen
    • Then log in to your Apple ID using the button at the top
